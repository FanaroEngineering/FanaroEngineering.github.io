Scripted Classes vs Consulting or Private Lessons

It's 2021 and we are still stuck with the same educational system and logic from more than 200 years ago. Everyone complains, everyone criticizes it, and we still can't seem to move on.

Most teachers I asked about this topic actually agree with me, but what can they do, really? They are mostly cogs on the machine, and they all depend on the system for their livelihoods, so it's comprehensible when they don't do anything about it.

But giving out the same lectures year after sets "understandable" as the ceiling for the situation of the educational system and teachers worldwide. That wouldn't be the case in the 1950s of course, however, ignoring the massive change which the ability of recording lectures has imparted seems beyond absurd and illogical to me.
