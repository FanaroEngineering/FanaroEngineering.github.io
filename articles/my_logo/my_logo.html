How I created my logo

The idea behind my logo came to me after struggling with the infinitely fragmented knowledge we typically have in schools and universities.

\[\# \ of \ Connections = C_{n, 2} = \frac{n!}{2!(n - 2)!} = \frac{n (n - 1)}{2}, \ for \ n \geq 3\]

\[\# \ of \ Internal \ Connections = C_{n,2} - n = \frac{n(n - 1)}{2} - n = \frac{n(n - 3)}{2}, \ for \ n \geq 3\]
