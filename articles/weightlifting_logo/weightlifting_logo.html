A Weightlifting Logo

After seeing my previous logo designs — my and the logos —, my Olympic Weightlifting coach, Rogerio Sousa, asked me if I could make a logo for him.

Initially, he gave me a picture with insufficient contrast, which would be a lot of work for me, since the Image Trace from Adobe Illustrator wouldn't be able to work that well. And, thus, I asked him for a picture with more lighting; Image Trace works better when there is great contrast for making icons, i.e., extracting the most iconic features of the image.

I suggested he took a picture similar to one in which Dmitry Klokov, and others, are holding the barbell and the plates trying to emulate a warrior's pose.

I've shared the design process on Instagram, but you can also check it out on YouTube. As a bonus, I've managed to make the snatch logo originally on his T-Shirt more abstract and, thus, creating a new logo in and of itself.
